Welcome Family

You are cordially invited to attend the 2022 Family Reunion. We have a variety of activities planned throughout the weekend. Come enjoy food, family, and fun as we celebrate each other and our family heritage.

The registration fee for the family reunion is $100 per person. This will include food, beverage, and activities during all scheduled events. Children under 3 do not require a registration fee. There are family reunion t-shirts available to purchase for the event. Fees and t-shirt purchases can be made through the event coordinator.

Our reunion will kick off with the “Life's a Beach” party. The family will enjoy a Meet & Greet with food, beverages, and beach activities. Everyone is encouraged to be in attendance. Don't forget to bring sunblock! Weekend events will also include a family BBQ in the beautiful Balboa Park. There will be a bouncy house and inflatable water slide available for the kids to enjoy. We'll also have an after-hours adult mixer after the children are put to bed. There will be a brunch send-off party on Sunday where we can enjoy breakfast and mimosas on the patio where you can share contact information with relatives, if you haven’t already.

We will have photo booth setup throughout the weekend to capture memories as we celebrate. This will be a great way to reconnect with close and distant relatives.

Our goal is to ensure that you all enjoy your visit to sunny San Diego. We cannot wait to see everyone!!
